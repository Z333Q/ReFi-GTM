## Primary ICP: Power-Retail Trader

### Demographics
- **Age:** 30-50 years old
- **Income:** $100K-$500K annually
- **Location:** UAE, GCC countries (initial focus)
- **Education:** Bachelor's degree or higher
- **Profession:** Tech, finance, engineering, consulting

### Trading Profile
- **Experience:** 5-10 years active trading
- **Account Size:** $50K-$250K
- **Trading Style:** Momentum, swing trading, options
- **Frequency:** 5-15 trades per week
- **Current Performance:** Inconsistent, Sharpe < 1.5

### Psychographics
- **Tech-Savvy:** Comfortable with APIs, wallets, crypto
- **Analytical:** Enjoys data, backtesting, optimization
- **Time-Constrained:** Has full-time job, can't monitor markets 24/7
- **Risk-Aware:** Post-FTX, custody-paranoid
- **Improvement-Oriented:** Reads trading books, takes courses

### Pain Points (Hypothesized)
1. Emotional trading destroys discipline
2. Time-intensive manual process (15-20 hrs/week)
3. Lack of institutional-grade tools
4. Custody risk with centralized platforms

### Willingness to Pay (Hypothesized)
- **Low:** $50-100/month
- **Medium:** $150-300/month
- **High:** $350-500/month
- **Hypothesis:** Will pay $300-500/month if proven performance

## Secondary ICP: Small Fund Manager (Future)

### Demographics
- **Age:** 35-55 years old
- **Role:** Founder, PM, CIO
- **Location:** Global (Singapore, US, Europe, UAE)
- **AUM:** $100M-$800M

### Firm Profile
- **Team Size:** 3-8 people
- **Strategy:** Quant, quant-overlay, systematic
- **Current Tech:** Fragmented (Bloomberg, FactSet, Python scripts)
- **Budget:** $200-500K/year on data + infrastructure

### Pain Points (Hypothesized)
1. Too small to afford $1M+ quant infrastructure
2. Too sophisticated for retail tools
3. "Awkward middle" gap in market

### Willingness to Pay (Hypothesized)
- **Hypothesis:** $5-10K/month base + 5 bps AUM

## Anti-ICP (Who We're NOT Targeting)

1. **Complete Beginners** (<2 years experience)
   - Don't understand risk management
   - Need education, not automation
   
2. **Buy-and-Hold Investors** (passive)
   - Don't actively trade
   - Robo-advisors serve them well

3. **High-Frequency Traders** (millisecond latency)
   - Need co-location, ultra-low latency
   - We're not optimized for HFT

4. **Traders with <$10K accounts**
   - Commissions eat into returns
   - Need to build capital first
